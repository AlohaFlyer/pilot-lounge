The Student arc, day one to first solo

Tex, Pualani, Chester, Otto, Andy, and John walk a brand new student from the discovery flight to the morning they fly alone: the dream and the first lessons, the traditions that make you one of us, home sims and study habits, then presolo knowledge, safety and responsibility, the community that lifts you, the careers ahead, and why we do things the way we do. Facts trace to the PHAK, AFH, ACS, and 14 CFR Parts 61 and 91. Stories are character color, not checkride facts.
